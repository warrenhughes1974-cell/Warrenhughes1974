## Summary

Release **v57.40** implements approved QuikLoan mapping v1.2: LifePRO `PLOAN` → QLAdmin `QuikLoan`. Gross loan balance is carried from `LOAN_BALANCE`; accrued interest is **not** calculated at conversion (`MLOANACCR=0.00`) — QLAdmin calculates interest after load.

Also preserved in this release line: **#27** (SL suppression), **#21D**, **#21J**, **#21K** (closed), **#21M / #21M-FU**, **#25, #26, #28, #31**.

**QuikLoan batch emit is opt-in** (`QLA_ENABLE_QUIKLOAN_EMIT=1`) until client UAT confirms QLAdmin interest calculation on trace policy `010331768C`.
