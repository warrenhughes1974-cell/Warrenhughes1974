EXECUTIVE FINDING
-----------------
Policy 010331768C (LifePRO 9010331768) demonstrates a material domain-routing conflict
between the current LifePRO→claims pipeline and QLAdmin target semantics.

LifePRO posts recurring 451↔412 "Loan Interest Capitalized" accounting entries.
The pipeline converts these into eight annual QUIKCLMS rows labeled Surrendered
(CLAIMSTAT=99) with MINTAMT populated and MPAID=0, while QUIKCLMP remains empty
and the policy stays active.

Per QLAdmin Help (authoritative target specification), recurring loan interest
accrual/capitalization belongs in the POLICY LOAN domain (QuikLoan, Loan History,
Coverage Tab loan fields) — NOT in QUIKCLMS/QUIKCLMP as repeated surrender claims.

VERDICT: SEMANTICALLY MISALIGNED — governance hold recommended pending business
sign-off on a new non-claim loan-maintenance classification path.


QLADMIN MANUAL — DOMAIN OWNERSHIP (CITED)
-----------------------------------------

1) QUIKCLMS = DEATH CLAIMS (not loan accounting)
   Source: QLAdmin Help PDF, Section 7.67 "QuikClms - Death Claims" (p.733)
   Table title in manual: "QuikClms - Death Claims"
   Key fields:
     - CLAIMSTAT: Pending-1; Paid in Full-2; Settled-3; Denied-4; Matured-98;
                  Surrenders-99
     - DTOFDEATH: Date of death
     - MINTAMT: "Amount of interest earned" (claim-benefit interest context)
     - LOAN: "Loan balance" (deduction on a claim settlement, not loan posting)
     - MPAID: "Amount paid"
   Interpretation: QUIKCLMS stores death claim and surrender claim transaction
   headers after Policy Maintenance claim/surrender processing — not internal
   loan ledger capitalization cycles on active policies.

2) Claims Tab scope (UI business semantics)
   Source: QLAdmin Help PDF, Policy Display §5.1.1.6 (p.44, p.48)
   Manual text: "Displays all death claim and surrender data for a life policy"
   and "All pending and finalized death claims, cash surrenders, or health claim
   details will be displayed in the Claims Tab."
   Claims Tab fields include Status, Death, Reported, Completed, Paid, Net Payment,
   Disbursement code, Check #, Payee.
   Interpretation: Claims Tab entries represent benefit settlement events with
   claim/surrender lifecycle — not annual loan interest accrual on active policies.

3) QUIKCLMP = DEATH CLAIM PAYEE DETAILS
   Source: QLAdmin Help PDF, Section 7.67 "QuikClmp - Death Claim Payee Details" (p.733)
   Death claim workflow requires Payee Transaction completion (p.116-117).
   Interpretation: Absence of QUIKCLMP rows for 010331768C is consistent with
   non-claim loan events; presence of QUIKCLMS surrender rows without payees is
   inconsistent with QLAdmin finalized claim/surrender behavior.

4) QuikLoan = POLICY LOANS (authoritative home for loan capitalization)
   Source: QLAdmin Help PDF, Section 7.150 "QuikLoan - Policy Loans" (p.827-828)
   Key fields (manual field dictionary):
     - MLOANPRIN: Loan principal
     - MLOANBAL: Loan balance
     - MLOANINT: Loan interest rate
     - MLOANINTX: Loan interest type (A=advance, R=arrears)
     - MLOANIDT: Interest paid to date
     - MLOANDATE: Loan balance date
     - MLOANACCR: Accrued interest
     - MLOANBILL: Billing loan payment
   Interpretation: Recurring capitalized loan interest semantically belongs here,
   not in QUIKCLMS.MINTAMT.

5) Loan History / Policy Maintenance Loan processing
   Source: QLAdmin Help PDF, §5.1.2.7 Loan History (p.74-75); §5.1.3.7 Processing
   a Loan (p.130-132)
   Manual text: "Loan transactions include loans granted, loan payments, loan
   interest charged, and automatic premium loans processed."
   "Current loan information is shown and includes the accrued interest, the
   loan balance and the date to which interest is accrued."
   "Which tables are affected? QuikLoan Policy Loans"
   Edit Loan: "Any adjustment in the loan balance will be recorded as capitalized
   interest on the loan, whether the adjustment is positive or negative."
   Interpretation: LifePRO 451↔412 capitalization maps to QLAdmin loan maintenance,
   not claim creation.

6) Anniversary Processing
   Source: QLAdmin Help PDF, Anniversary Processing overview (p.26)
   Manual text includes: "Process policy loans and capitalize interest."
   Interpretation: Annual loan interest capitalization is scheduled policy/loan
   processing — aligned with observed Jul-24 recurrence on 010331768C.

7) QuikBlll — Billing Loan Interest (operational, non-claim)
   Source: QLAdmin Help PDF, §7.58 QuikBlll (p.722-723)
   Field MLOANBILL: "Amount billed for policy loan"
   Interpretation: Loan interest may also surface in billing notices — still not
   in QUIKCLMS.

ROOT CAUSE (PIPELINE vs MANUAL)
-------------------------------
Phase 4 FAMILY_GROUPS maps LOAN_INTEREST_OFFSET (0412) into SURRENDER_CLAIM.
Phase 10B applies SURRENDER claimstat_default=99 even when lifecycle=INFERRED.
Phase 6 balancing treats offset-only chains as BALANCED without requiring
surrender funding/payout codes (0560/0561/0090/1020).
Phase 17 governance clears on reconciliation PASS without QLAdmin domain alignment.

The QLAdmin manual explicitly separates:
  - Loan interest charged / capitalized → QuikLoan, Loan History, Anniversary
  - Death claims / cash surrenders → Claims Tab, QUIKCLMS, QUIKCLMP

Current routing collapses these domains.
